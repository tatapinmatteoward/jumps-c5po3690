## Building Your Weekly JumpStats Routine

Start with a balanced structure. Here's a sample 4-day integration plan:

- **Day 1: Assessment & Warm-Up (Monday)**
  Use JumpStats Pro for a baseline jump test. Focus on vertical jumps to gauge your starting point. Review metrics like power output and ensure proper form via the app's video analysis.

- **Day 2: Explosive Power Session (Wednesday)**
  Incorporate plyometrics tracked by JumpStats. Aim for 3-5 sets of box jumps or depth jumps. Post-session, analyze reactive strength index (RSI) to measure improvement.

- **Day 3: Recovery & Mobility (Friday)**
  Light session with JumpStats' asymmetry checks. Monitor landing impacts to spot imbalances early, preventing overuse injuries.

- **Day 4: Peak Performance Drill (Sunday)**
  High-intensity interval jumps. Use the app's progress charts to celebrate gains and adjust for the next week.
