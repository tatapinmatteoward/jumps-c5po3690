## Why Advanced Metrics Matter
In the world of sports like basketball, volleyball, and track & field, a superior vertical jump can make all the difference. But random plyometric drills? That's old school. JumpStats Pro uses cutting-edge sensors and AI algorithms to deliver metrics like:

- **Reactive Strength Index (RSI)**: Measures how quickly you apply force post-landing. Higher RSI means faster, more powerful jumps.
- **Jump Efficiency Score**: Tracks energy transfer from ground contact to takeoff, helping you fine-tune technique.
- **Power Output Variability**: Identifies inconsistencies in your sessions, pinpointing fatigue or form breakdowns.

These aren't gimmicks; they're backed by sports science, helping athletes at all levels—from weekend warriors to pros—achieve measurable gains.

## Real-World Application: A Training Case Study
